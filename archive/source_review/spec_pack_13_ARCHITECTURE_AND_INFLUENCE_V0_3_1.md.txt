Player / Dev Freedom Goals
By enforcing a strict physical substrate (Vec<Cell>), we guarantee that the world is the interface. The player doesn't manage numbers on a spreadsheet; they manage physical space, structural integrity, and biological traces. This substrate gives developers freedom to add emergent features without rewriting abstract zone-logic, and it gives players organic consequence rather than card-game menus.
