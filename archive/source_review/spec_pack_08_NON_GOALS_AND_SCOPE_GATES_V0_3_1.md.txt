08 — Non-Goals and Scope Gates
Hard Non-Goals
Do not build:
procedural world generation
card-dashboard gameplay
Unreal Engine integration
GPU-owned compute logic
multiplayer
full packet campaign layer
A* pathfinding
full food economy
doctrine system
Deep Queen layer
spider/centipede arcs
complete biology simulation (keep Sourback as residue/pressure only)
Scope Gate
A proposed feature belongs in Chunk 0 only if it helps prove at least one of: material cell behavior, digging, collapse, moisture, scent, residue, carcass harvesting, ant-group interaction, perception split, or deterministic replay.
Language Guardrails
Player-facing text says "bitter/yellow residue" before "Sourback path" is earned. The UI must not leak hidden canon prematurely.
