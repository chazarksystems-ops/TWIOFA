02 — Numeric Chunk Layout and ASCII Map
Seeded Layout Contract
For Chunk 0, the initial layout is fully hardcoded to these exact specifications. Procedural generation is explicitly banned.
Boundary Behavior
x=0, x=127, y=0, y=127 are treated as solid boundaries.
Water stops at edges.
Ants cannot leave the 128x128 chunk.
No wrapping.
Exact Coordinate Bands (128x128)
Coordinate ranges are half-open: x_start..x_end includes x_start and excludes x_end. y_start..y_end includes y_start and excludes y_end.
