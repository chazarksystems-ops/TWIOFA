# 13 — Architecture and Influence Map

## Player / Developer Freedom Goals

The material substrate exists to make the world itself the interface.

Player freedom should come from physical action:

```text
dig
route
flood/dry
harvest
avoid
inspect
misread
learn
recover
```

Developer freedom should come from inspectable rules:

```text
modular materials
deterministic replay
simple rule tables
visible debugging
safe future expansion
```

## What TWIOFA Takes from Noita

| Noita influence | TWIOFA application | What not to copy |
|---|---|---|
| Pixel/material identity | Every cell can matter physically. | Do not build a full Noita clone first. |
| Emergent material consequences | Water, loose soil, scent, residue, carcass interact. | Do not require 60 FPS full-world fluid sim. |
| Player experimentation | Colony can dig/route/harvest/avoid. | Do not focus on single wizard/action combat. |
| Material world as source of surprise | Collapse, residue, moisture alter plans. | Do not make chaos unreadable. |

## What TWIOFA Takes from Diablo / Hades

| Influence | TWIOFA application | What not to copy |
|---|---|---|
| Spatial readability | Later rich yard camera and route readability. | Do not build this before substrate works. |
| Strong environmental presence | Yard should feel like a place. | Do not over-polish Chunk 0. |
| Fast tactical scan | Clear physical cues and overlays. | Do not reduce game to generic ARPG combat. |

## What TWIOFA Takes from BG3

| Influence | TWIOFA application | What not to copy |
|---|---|---|
| Rich inspection | Cell/region hover with evidence and confidence. | Do not expose omniscient hidden truth. |
| Consequence presentation | Command receipts and event ledger. | Do not become dialogue/card UI. |
| Tactical clarity | Player understands available actions. | Do not build party RPG mechanics. |

## TWIOFA Difference

Noita asks:

```text
What happens when materials and spells collide?
```

TWIOFA asks:

```text
What happens when a colony tries to survive inside a material world it only partially understands?
```

## Future Expansion Ladder

After Chunk 0 proves the material substrate:

1. **Chunk 1:** dirty-region tracking and multi-chunk edge rules.
2. **Chunk 2:** improved scent/pheromone fields and larger ant-group footprint.
3. **Chunk 3:** richer perception ledger / Field Notes integration.
4. **Chunk 4:** richer spatial camera / Unreal bridge exploration.
5. **Chunk 5:** GPU visual overlays only after CPU truth boundary is proven.

Do not skip directly to later chunks.
